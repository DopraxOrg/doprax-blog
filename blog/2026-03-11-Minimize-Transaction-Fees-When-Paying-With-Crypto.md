# Guide: Minimizing Transaction Fees When Paying with Cryptocurrency on Doprax

Paying for cloud services with cryptocurrency is a practical solution for users worldwide, offering a borderless way to manage hosting costs. However, transaction fees (often called "gas fees") can vary significantly depending on which blockchain network you use.

This guide explains the most cost-effective way to add credit to your Doprax account by choosing the right payment networks.

## Why Network Selection Matters

When you send cryptocurrency, the cost is determined by the network you select, not the amount you are sending. 

* **High-Fee Networks:** Using the Ethereum network (ERC-20) for stablecoins like USDT can often result in transaction fees ranging from $10 to $30 or more during busy periods.
* **Low-Fee Networks:** Using the **Tron network (TRX/TRC-20)** is currently one of the most efficient methods. Transactions on the Tron network typically cost around $1, regardless of the amount being transferred.

## How to Add Credit with Minimum Fees

Follow these steps to top up your account balance efficiently:

### 1. Access the Billing Dashboard
Log in to your [Doprax account](https://www.doprax.com/) and navigate to the **Billing** section from the main menu. Click on the **Add credit** button.

### 2. Enter the Amount
Specify the amount of USD credit you wish to add to your balance. Once entered, select the **Pay with crypto** option and click **Checkout**.

### 3. Select the Payment Provider
You will be redirected to the payment gateway. Choose **NowPayments** to proceed to the cryptocurrency selection screen.

### 4. Choose a Low-Fee Currency/Network
To minimize your overhead costs, we recommend selecting one of the following options within the NowPayments interface:
* **TRX (Tron):** Fast confirmation times and minimal fees.
* **USDT (TRC-20):** Ensure you select the **TRC-20** version (Tron network) rather than the ERC-20 version to avoid high Ethereum gas fees.
* **Polygon (POL/MATIC):** Another excellent low-cost alternative for fast transactions.

### 5. Complete the Transaction
The system will display a unique wallet address and the exact amount of cryptocurrency required. 
* Copy the address or scan the QR code.
* Send the funds from your personal wallet or exchange.
* **Important:** Ensure you are sending via the **correct network**. For example, if you selected TRC-20, your wallet must also be set to send via the Tron (TRC-20) network.

## Final Confirmation
Once the transaction is broadcast to the blockchain, you can monitor the status on the payment page. After the required number of network confirmations is reached, the credit will be applied to your Doprax account automatically.

By choosing high-efficiency networks like Tron or Polygon, you ensure that the vast majority of your payment goes directly toward your cloud resources rather than network fees.
